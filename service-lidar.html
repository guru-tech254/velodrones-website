Seeing What Others Miss

Optical cameras can only see what's visible from above. When dense vegetation, power lines, or complex infrastructure obscure the ground, traditional photogrammetry fails — leaving you with incomplete data and costly blind spots.

Velodrones' LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) technology changes everything. Our airborne laser scanners fire 500,000 pulses per second, penetrating gaps in foliage to capture the true bare-earth surface beneath. The result is a comprehensive 3D point cloud that reveals terrain, structures, and vegetation structure simultaneously — with 5cm vertical accuracy that meets the strictest engineering standards.

LiDAR Deliverables

  • Classified 3D Point Clouds (LAS/LAZ format) — automatically segmented into Ground, Vegetation, Buildings, and Power Lines
  • Bare-Earth Digital Terrain Models (DTM) — accurate ground surface beneath all vegetation
  • Digital Surface Models (DSM) — including all features (trees, buildings, infrastructure)
  • Canopy Height Models (CHM) — for forestry applications and biomass estimation
  • Contour lines and breaklines — at specified intervals for engineering design
  • Cross-sections and profile views — for corridor projects and road design
  • Vegetation encroachment reports — with clearance violations and risk assessments

Why LiDAR is Non-Negotiable

When accuracy under vegetation or in complex environments is critical, LiDAR is the only solution. Our airborne LiDAR surveys deliver 5cm vertical accuracy across challenging terrain that would be impossible to survey from the ground — saving you weeks of dangerous fieldwork and eliminating costly data gaps.

For utilities, transportation agencies, and engineering firms, this means safer designs, fewer surprises during construction, and significantly reduced project risk. One LiDAR survey prevents millions in change orders and delays.
