Aerial Surveillance

Real-time and scheduled aerial monitoring for large-scale sites, events, and critical perimeter security. We provide persistent situational awareness from above, enhancing safety and operational control without ground-based blind spots.

Persistent Eyes in the Sky

Ground-based security cameras have limited range and fixed perspectives. Manned patrols are slow and can't cover vast areas effectively. Velodrones' aerial surveillance solutions provide dynamic, real-time visibility over entire facilities, event venues, or critical infrastructure corridors.

Using long-endurance UAV platforms equipped with EO/IR (Electro-Optical/Infra-Red) gimbals and live video downlink systems, we deliver continuous aerial coverage that enhances existing security operations. Our pilots can respond instantly to incidents, track moving targets, and provide command centers with actionable aerial intelligence.

Live Video Streaming

Real-time HD video feed transmitted directly to your command center or mobile devices for immediate situational awareness.

Thermal Night Operations

Infrared imaging for 24/7 surveillance, detecting intruders, hotspots, and anomalies invisible to the naked eye.

Event Crowd Monitoring

Overhead crowd density analysis, flow management, and emergency response coordination for large public gatherings.

Perimeter Security

Automated patrol routes along facility boundaries with AI-powered anomaly detection and instant alert notifications.

Search & Rescue Support

Rapid area coverage with thermal imaging to locate missing persons, stranded vehicles, or distress signals in difficult terrain.

Compliance Monitoring

Documented aerial verification of site conditions, safety protocol adherence, and environmental compliance.

Surveillance Capabilities

  • Live HD video streaming with low-latency downlink
  • Thermal/IR imaging for night and low-visibility operations
  • Zoom capabilities for detailed identification at distance
  • GPS-tagged incident recording and timestamped evidence
  • Automated waypoint patrols for consistent coverage
  • Integration with existing security command centers
  • Post-flight analysis reports and annotated footage

Whether protecting critical infrastructure, managing large events, or supporting emergency response, our aerial surveillance solutions give you the upper hand with unmatched situational awareness.
