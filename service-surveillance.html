Persistent Eyes in the Sky

Ground-based security cameras have limited range and fixed perspectives. Manned patrols are slow, expensive, and can't cover vast areas effectively. Blind spots become vulnerabilities — and vulnerabilities become incidents.

Velodrones' aerial surveillance solutions provide dynamic, real-time visibility over entire facilities, event venues, or critical infrastructure corridors. Using long-endurance UAV platforms equipped with EO/IR (Electro-Optical/Infra-Red) gimbals and live video downlink systems, we deliver continuous aerial coverage that enhances existing security operations. Our pilots respond instantly to incidents, track moving targets, and provide command centers with actionable aerial intelligence — day or night.
