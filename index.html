LiDAR Surveying

See through vegetation. Capture what cameras can't.

When dense canopy hides the ground beneath, optical cameras fail. Our airborne LiDAR fires 500,000 laser pulses per second, penetrating foliage to reveal the true terrain below. It's the only technology that delivers bare-earth accuracy in forests, jungles, and overgrown corridors — critical for power line routing, flood modeling, and road design.
