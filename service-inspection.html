Industrial Inspections

Safe, detailed assessment of towers, pipelines, bridges, and powerlines without scaffolding or operational shutdowns. We deliver engineering-grade visual intelligence that keeps your assets running and your people safe.

Why Choose Drone-Based Industrial Inspections?

Traditional inspection methods require expensive scaffolding, rope access teams, crane rentals, and often force costly operational shutdowns. Velodrones replaces these outdated approaches with advanced UAV technology that captures millimeter-level detail from every angle — safely, quickly, and without disrupting your operations.

Our enterprise-grade drones are equipped with high-resolution optical zoom cameras, thermal imaging sensors, and RTK positioning systems that deliver data accurate enough for engineering decisions. Every inspection is conducted by certified pilots following strict aviation safety protocols.

Powerline & Transmission Inspections

Detailed visual and thermal assessment of towers, conductors, insulators, and hardware without de-energizing lines or deploying ground crews.

Pipeline & Corridor Surveys

Comprehensive aerial inspection of pipeline routes, identifying leaks, corrosion, encroachments, and right-of-way issues across hundreds of kilometers.

Bridge & Structural Assessments

High-resolution imaging of bridge undersides, pylons, cables, and expansion joints — areas that are dangerous or impossible to reach manually.

Plant & Facility Inspections

Roof assessments, stack inspections, flare stack monitoring, and confined space evaluations without shutting down production.

Wind Turbine Inspections

Blade surface analysis, leading-edge erosion detection, and structural integrity assessments at heights of 100+ meters — safely and efficiently.

Solar Farm Audits

Thermal imaging to detect faulty panels, hot spots, and underperforming modules across large-scale solar installations.

What You Receive

  • High-resolution orthomosaic imagery of the entire asset
  • Thermal analysis reports identifying anomalies and defects
  • Georeferenced defect maps with precise GPS coordinates
  • 3D models and digital twins for long-term asset monitoring
  • Comprehensive inspection reports with prioritized recommendations
  • Before/after comparison data for maintenance tracking
  • Compliance-ready documentation for regulatory submissions
