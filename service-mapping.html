Drone Mapping & Surveying

High-accuracy topographical surveys delivering Orthomosaics, DEMs, DTMs, and DSMs for precise engineering. We replace slow ground surveys with rapid aerial data capture that meets survey-grade standards.

Precision Aerial Data Capture

Traditional land surveying is slow, labor-intensive, and often misses critical details in inaccessible terrain. Velodrones utilizes RTK/PPK-enabled UAV platforms equipped with high-resolution RGB sensors to capture millions of georeferenced data points in a single flight.

Our processed deliverables provide sub-5cm absolute accuracy, enabling engineers and planners to make confident decisions based on current, comprehensive site data rather than outdated or incomplete information.

Orthomosaic Maps

Georeferenced, distortion-free aerial imagery stitched into seamless, high-resolution maps perfect for planning, design, and documentation.

Digital Elevation Models (DEM)

Accurate representation of bare-earth terrain, essential for flood modeling, drainage analysis, and earthwork calculations.

3D Point Clouds

Dense, measurable 3D representations of sites and structures, compatible with CAD and BIM software for detailed analysis.

Volumetric Analysis

Precise calculation of stockpile volumes, cut-and-fill quantities, and earthwork balances for construction and mining projects.

Progress Monitoring

Recurring mapping flights to track construction progress, validate contractor claims, and maintain accurate as-built records.

Large Area Surveys

Efficiently map hundreds of hectares in hours, covering vast distances that would take ground crews weeks to survey.

Deliverables You Receive

  • Survey-grade Orthomosaic maps (GeoTIFF format)
  • Digital Surface Models (DSM) & Digital Terrain Models (DTM)
  • High-density 3D Point Clouds (LAS/LAZ format)
  • Contour lines and breaklines for engineering design
  • Volumetric reports with cross-sections
  • GIS-compatible shapefiles and KML overlays
  • Comprehensive accuracy reports and GCP documentation
