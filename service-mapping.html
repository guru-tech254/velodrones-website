The Mapping Revolution

Traditional land surveying is slow, expensive, and often misses critical details in inaccessible terrain. While your competitors wait weeks for ground crews to trudge through the bush, our RTK-enabled drones capture millions of georeferenced data points in a single morning — with accuracy that meets the strictest engineering standards.

Whether you need a baseline for a new development, progress tracking for a mega-project, or volumetrics for a quarry, we deliver engineering-ready orthomosaics, DEMs, DTMs, and 3D point clouds faster and more cost-effectively than any ground-based alternative.

Deliverables You Receive

  • Survey-grade Orthomosaic maps (GeoTIFF format) — pixel-perfect, georeferenced imagery ready for CAD/GIS
  • Digital Surface Models (DSM) & Digital Terrain Models (DTM) — accurate elevation data for engineering design
  • High-density 3D Point Clouds (LAS/LAZ format) — millions of measurable points for detailed analysis
  • Contour lines and breaklines — ready for engineering design and earthwork calculations
  • Volumetric reports with cross-sections — precise stockpile and cut/fill calculations
  • GIS-compatible shapefiles and KML overlays — seamless integration with your existing workflows
  • Comprehensive accuracy reports — full GCP documentation and QA/QC verification
